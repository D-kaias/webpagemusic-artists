🎵 Artist Profile

Artist Name

Taylor Swift is an American singer-songwriter born on December 13, 1989, in West Reading, Pennsylvania, who has become one of the best-selling and most influential artists in history. She began her career as a country artist in 2006 with her self-titled debut album before transitioning to pop with 1989 in 2014, and later exploring indie folk and synth-pop in subsequent years.
